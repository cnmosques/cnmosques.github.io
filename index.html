Introduction of China Mosques

The latest statistics of 2014 show that China has 39,135 mosques and Islamic activity venues.Many of them are historical and beautiful,here let me introduce them to you.

Beijing

Beijing has 70 mosques, Niujie Mosque, Dongsi Mosque, mosque goers, Madian Mosque, Tongzhou Mosque and the regular business Mosque is the most famous of them.

Zhejiang

Zhejiang has 5 mosques, Hangzhou Phoenix Mosque is the most famous of them.
